About me

After graduating at the top of my class, winning multiple awards, in Software Engineering at the HEIG-VD in Yverdon-les-Bains, Switzerland, I worked at the Digger Foundation to complete my mandatory national service. Since then, I continued to work with them to contribute toward removing anti-personal mines from the world and worked on personal programming projects.

My interests are AI and evolution, user interfaces and software architectures. I believe that users are often stuck with unusable programs : I want to change that and make great tools that can be used seamlessly by their users.

Currently looking for a software engineering job in a company that makes great and interesting products!
